Jaa Func Trivia Game

Let's Learn the Jaa Func Way

Jaa Func is a trivia game that keeps score and allows you to learn and have fun doing it.

1. First start by answering the question in the display screen. 2. Than hit the answer choices to get a multiple choice. 3.Next, reveal the answer choice and simply hit a or b, if the answer is correct than you will get a score of ten but if the wrong answer you get no points at all.

Then move to the next question by hitting the next question button once. If you score from 60-100 points you win otherwise you lose!
