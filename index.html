Let's Learn the Jaa Func Way

Jaa Func is a trivia game that keeps score and allows you to learn and have fun doing it.

1. First start by answering the question in the display screen. 2. Than hit the answer choices to get a multiple choice. 3.Next, there's an answer dropdown to choose the right answer, if the answer is correct than you will get a score of ten.

To move to the next question hit the next question button and repeat the instructions in 1, 2, and 3. Make sure you submit your answer choices after you finish every answer to see if you win or loose!
